hello! I'm James Eschrich, a PhD student at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign.

I work with Dr. Sarah Sterman in the Process, Interaction, and Creativity Lab (PICL), researching writing and creativity. I am particularly interested in studying the situated, embodied, and historical aspects of the creative process, and in how to give communities of creative practitioners more agency over the design, evolution, and use of Creativity Support Tools.

As a Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) researcher, I draw on my interdisciplinary background in Computer Science, English Literature, mathematics, and creative writing.
